# Do you think that's air you're breathing now?


Hey, Hope you are having a good day.

I am a `Cognitive Scientist` at the Centre for Consciousness Studies, NIMHANS,
and an engineer-turned-neuroscientist. With over a decade of experience across
`working memory` `neuromodulation` `meditation` and `sleep` research. I enjoy continuous learning in general, reading, good food, and traveling occasionally
to observe the changes in the world. Recently, I have developed an interest in
physical fitness and breath work.

I am establishing a research niche around **Sense of Self** exploring how
it emerges with a rich phenomenology and how it fragments in various states 
such as schizophrenia, autism, depression and lucid dreaming.
This work integrates neuroscience, psychiatry, technology, and contemplative
science to understand the shifts of selfhood and their impact on mental health.

On a typical day, I spend half my time on Sense of Self research. The other half is scattered across helping out with cool projects happening at the Centre for Consciousness Studies mostly tackling technical, signal processing, and statistical problems.

I also mentor and lead a couple of intern-led projects, where we play around with datasets exploring new methods and concepts: Brain Age Index from resting EEG, improving how Heart-Evoked Potentials are captured across various states like sleep, task, and meditation, and foundational models with EEG.
On top of that, I teach some core fundamentals that I believe can have meaningful impact across lab members and contribute to broader upskilling beyond academia.

I care deeply about how science is conducted and loathe the obsession with
impact factors and pseudo-prestige in academia. I believe in flat hierarchies,
open science and the elimination of subtle "-isms".
I am a proponent of [deep work](https://calnewport.com/some-notes-on-deep-working/) and I value mental health and highly value physical-mental health, relationships, and core values.
I enjoy working with people who are self driven, possess a growth mindset and
are reflective and collaborative. If you are someone with a similar thought process and/or want to work with me,please send an email to me at `rhlvenugopal@gmail.com`
